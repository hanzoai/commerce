Introduction

Welcome to our service

Thank you for your interest in Crowdstart!

Crowdstart is an infinitely scalable crowdfunding & pre-order platform.

Who are we?

We are Crowdstart, founded and developed by the awesome guys at Verus Media.

In case you aren't familiar with Verus Media, they're crowdfunding marketing specialists that grew tired with the shortcomings of all other crowdfunding platforms that they've managed campaigns on.

What we believe in?
We believe that when a contributor backs your campaign, no one should be holding onto that money limiting access to funds that should be going to delivering the product you promised.

We also believe that companies need more than just a place that accepts orders - there is a lot more to it than that, such as order management, fulfillment and customer service. We've made it easy for you to complete this project beyond just accepting orders.

Functionality

Is your application responsive?
Our platform is responsive in design so it can be viewed optimally from any device.
Is my data secured?
We've ensured that all aspects of your data will be as secure as possible, using SSL encryption technologies.
How often do you release new features?
We are in a constant state of development, if you are interested in signing up to be a part of our beta tester group which get to test out the latest features before we implement into production, submit an email to beta@hanzo.io.
Can I suggest new features?
Sign-up for the beta tester group and which gives you access to our feature request submission section of our site.
